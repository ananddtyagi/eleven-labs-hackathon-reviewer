Creativity and Innovation (25%):

Pitch and Presentation (25%):
- Presentation Weaknesses:

  * Missing information about cost structure and business model

  * No discussion of potential risks or limitations

2. Market Viability:

3. Implementation Challenges:
   - Complex technical stack may lead to frequent failures
   - High maintenance requirements

Recommendations for Improvement:
1. Develop robust content moderation systems
2. Implement comprehensive security measures
3. Add more interactive elements
