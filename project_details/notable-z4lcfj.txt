<h2>Inspiration</h2>
<p>Customer feedback is a goldmine of insights, but reviewing recorded calls is tedious and time-consuming. Executives and teams spend hours sifting through conversations to find critical information. We wanted to create a solution that makes customer feedback more accessible, engaging, and actionableâ€”turning hours of calls into digestible, insightful content.  </p>
<h2>What It Does</h2>
<p>Notable is an AI agent that transforms recorded customer calls into engaging, easy-to-consume podcasts for executives. It seamlessly integrates with call recording platforms, automatically analyzes key moments, and generates a podcast summarizing the most valuable insights. This allows teams to stay informed without the hassle of manual call reviews.  </p>
<h2>How We Built It</h2>
<p>We used:  </p>
<ul>
<li>React: for the frontend to create an intuitive and seamless user experience.<br/></li>
<li>Supabase: for database management and authentication</li>
<li>Supabase Edge functions: for text to speech and podcast script generation</li>
<li>ElevenLabs: for high-quality AI-generated voice narration.<br/></li>
<li>OpenAI: to analyze call transcripts, extract insights, and generate summaries.<br/></li>
<li>Serverless architecture: to ensure scalability and efficiency.<br/></li>
</ul>
