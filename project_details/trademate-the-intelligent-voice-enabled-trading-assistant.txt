<h1>TradeMate ‚Äì The Intelligent Voice-Enabled Trading Assistant</h1>
<h3>Summary</h3>
<p>TradeMate is a AI Agent designed to assist traders‚Äîwhether beginners or experienced‚Äîby combining real-time market analysis, customizable voice notifications, an interactive AI chat, and a functional simulated trading system.  </p>
<p>Developed solo for the ElevenLabs x a16z Hackathon, this project leverages <strong>Next.js with TypeScript</strong> for a high-performance interface, the <strong>Binance API</strong> for market data, <strong>OpenAI</strong> for AI logic, and <strong>ElevenLabs</strong> for realistic voice outputs in English and French.  </p>
<p>TradeMate delivers an immersive, accessible experience, perfect for busy or mobile traders.</p>

<h2>Key Features</h2>
<h3>1. Real-Time Market Analysis</h3>
<ul>
<li>TradeMate displays an <strong>interactive Bitcoin (BTC) chart</strong> in real-time, powered by the Binance API.<br/></li>
<li>Key information includes:

<ul>
<li><strong>Current price</strong></li>
<li><strong>1-hour price variation</strong></li>
<li><strong>Trading volume</strong></li>
</ul></li>
<li>Data is dynamically updated to reflect market fluctuations, providing users with a clear and accurate overview.</li>
</ul>
<h3>2. Intelligent Voice Notifications with ElevenLabs</h3>
<ul>
<li>A <strong>realistic AI-generated voice</strong> informs users at customizable intervals (e.g., every 5, 10, or 15 minutes) about market conditions.</li>
<li>Voice notifications include:

<ul>
<li><strong>Current BTC price</strong><br/></li>
<li><strong>1-hour variation</strong> (e.g., <em>‚ÄúThe price of Bitcoin is $50,000, with a +2.5% variation over 1 hour‚Äù</em>).</li>
</ul></li>
<li>Users can <strong>select from multiple voice models</strong> (e.g., Alice, Bill, Aria) and <strong>two languages</strong> (English/French).<br/></li>
<li><strong>Automatic voice mode</strong> can be toggled on/off via the interface.</li>
</ul>
<h3>3. Interactive AI Chat with OpenAI</h3>
<ul>
<li>An <strong>integrated chat</strong>, powered by OpenAI, allows users to ask questions about finance, the market, or trading strategies.<br/>
<ul>
<li>Example: <em>‚ÄúWhat‚Äôs the best time to buy Bitcoin?‚Äù</em> ‚Üí AI provides insights.<br/></li>
</ul></li>
<li>Chat responses can also be <strong>enhanced with voice notifications</strong> from ElevenLabs.<br/></li>
<li>Future improvements could expand this feature for more complex interactions.</li>
</ul>
<h3>4. Functional Simulated Trading with AI</h3>
<ul>
<li>TradeMate includes a <strong>simulated trading system</strong>, where OpenAI‚Äôs AI:

<ul>
<li>Analyzes market data<br/></li>
<li>Applies <strong>fictional positions</strong> (e.g., buying or selling BTC) based on simple rules (<em>e.g., buy if the 1-hour variation exceeds +2%</em>).<br/></li>
</ul></li>
<li>Users can see a <strong>simulated balance updated in real-time</strong>, with voice confirmation from ElevenLabs (e.g., <em>‚ÄúPosition opened: Bought Bitcoin at $50,000‚Äù</em>).<br/></li>
<li>This system is <strong>purely educational</strong> and does not handle real money but <strong>faithfully simulates automated trading</strong>.</li>
</ul>
<h3>5. Modern User Interface with Next.js and TypeScript</h3>
<ul>
<li>Built with <strong>Next.js</strong> for optimal performance and <strong>TypeScript</strong> for robustness.</li>
<li>Features:

<ul>
<li><strong>Interactive chart</strong> (using Lightweight charts) visualizing BTC trends.</li>
<li><strong>Buttons &amp; menus</strong> for settings (e.g., voice, chat, simulated trading).</li>

</ul></li>
</ul>
