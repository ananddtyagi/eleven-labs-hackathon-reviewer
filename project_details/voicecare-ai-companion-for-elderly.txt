<h2>Inspiration</h2>
<p>Our project started with the thought that our elderly loved ones, who cared for us when we were young, deserve the same care and attention as they age. We saw that many seniors feel lonely and struggle with managing daily tasks like taking medication or keeping appointments. This sparked our idea to build an AI companion that can help them feel connected and supported.</p>
<h2>What it does</h2>
<p>VoiceCare is a friendly AI that talks with seniors and helps manage their daily routines. It reminds them to take their medication, helps set up appointments, offers fun conversations and even gives quick weather updates. In case of emergencies, it can alert caregivers with a simple voice command. In short, it keeps our elders company and helps them stay safe.</p>
<h2>How we built it</h2>
<p>We built VoiceCare using a mix of modern tools and technologies. Our system uses:</p>
<ul>
<li>Voice Processing &amp; Speech Recognition: Powered by Eleven Labs to make the conversation feels natural.</li>
<li>Communication: Twilio helps send quick SMS alerts in emergencies.</li>
<li>User Interface: We used Next.js, TypeScript, and Tailwind CSS to create a simple and friendly design.</li>
<li>Deployment: Vercel is powering our VoiceCare Engine.</li>
</ul>
<p>By combining these technologies, we made a tool that listens, understands, and responds to the needs of seniors.</p>
<h2>Challenges we ran into</h2>
<p>During development, we faced several challenges:</p>
<ul>
<li>Making it Simple: Designing a system that is easy for seniors to use required extra care in both language and interface design.</li>
<li>Voice Accuracy: Ensuring the voice recognition works well, even when speech is slow or soft, was difficult.</li>
<li>Integration Issues: Combining different APIs (for voice, SMS, and weather) required a lot of testing and fine-tuning.</li>
<li>Reliability: Building a tool that seniors can depend on, especially during emergencies, was our top priority and a significant challenge.</li>
</ul>
<h2>Accomplishments that we're proud of</h2>
<p>We are proud to have built a working MVP that:</p>
<ul>
<li>Successfully integrates multiple technologies into one easy-to-use platform.</li>
<li>Provides clear and friendly voice interactions tailored for elderly users.</li>
<li>Offers real-time medication reminders, appointment scheduling, and emergency alerts.</li>
<li>Opens up a new way to use AI for meaningful social good.</li>
</ul>
