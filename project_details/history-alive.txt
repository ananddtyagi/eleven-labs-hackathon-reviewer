<h1>ElevenLabs Hackathon: AI-Powered Historical Documentary Generator</h1>
<h2>Project Overview</h2>
<p>A system that brings historical paintings to life through AI-generated documentaries, combining ElevenLabs' state-of-the-art Text-to-Speech with fal.ai's video generation models. The project uses a multi-agent architecture to create engaging historical narratives directly from primary sources.</p>
