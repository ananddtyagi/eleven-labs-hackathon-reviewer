<h2>Inspiration</h2>
<p>Kids love hearing stories from their loved ones, but family members can’t always be there to tell them. Research shows that familiar voices enhance learning and emotional development, making storytelling even more meaningful.  </p>
<p>StorySage was created to bring <strong>personalized, AI-generated stories</strong> to children—anytime, in the voices they love.  </p>
<h2>What it does</h2>
<p>StorySage turns simple prompts into <strong>custom AI-generated story videos</strong>, narrated in a loved one’s voice. Parents, grandparents, or siblings can create immersive, heartwarming tales with just a few clicks.  </p>
<h2>How we built it</h2>
<ol>
<li><strong>Story Generation</strong> – GPT-4 writes an engaging, age-appropriate script.<br/></li>
<li><strong>Visuals &amp; Animation</strong> – FAL generates and animates story scenes.<br/></li>
<li><strong>Voice Cloning</strong> – Eleven Labs replicates a family member’s voice.<br/></li>
<li><strong>Assembly &amp; Streaming</strong> – VideoDB synchronizes everything for seamless playback.<br/></li>
</ol>
<h2>Challenges we faced</h2>

</ul>
