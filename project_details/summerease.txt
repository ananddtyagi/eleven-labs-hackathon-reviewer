<h2>Inspiration</h2>
<p>In today’s fast-paced world, staying informed can feel overwhelming. With so many news sources and a constant stream of information, it’s easy to miss out on what truly matters. We wanted to create a solution that makes news consumption effortless, accessible, and engaging.</p>
<p>The idea behind <strong>SummerEase</strong> was born from the need to simplify news delivery, offering users a convenient way to stay updated without reading long articles or scrolling through endless feeds. Inspired by podcasts and the growing trend of audio-based content, we envisioned a <strong>daily news assistant</strong> that delivers <strong>personalized audio updates</strong> directly to Telegram.</p>
<hr/>
<h2>What it does</h2>
<p><strong>SummerEase</strong> is a news companion bot that provides users with <strong>daily audio updates</strong> on their favorite topics.</p>
<p>By subscribing through our platform, users receive a <strong>curated selection of news</strong> in a <strong>short, digestible audio format</strong>, delivered directly to their <strong>Telegram at 9 AM Paris time</strong>.</p>
<h3>Key Features:</h3>
<ul>
<li>🎙️ <strong>Daily audio news updates</strong> tailored to individual preferences</li>
<li>🌍 <strong>Wide range of topics</strong> to choose from</li>
<li>🔄 <strong>Easy subscription management</strong> via simple registration</li>
<li>📲 <strong>Seamless Telegram integration</strong> for instant access</li>
<li>❌ <strong>Effortless unsubscribe option</strong> for user convenience</li>
</ul>
<hr/>
<h2>How we built it</h2>
<p>The development of <strong>SummerEase</strong> involved multiple technologies:</p>
<ul>
<li>⚙️ <strong>MAKE</strong> for the automatic workflow (<strong>GPT-4o, ElevenLabs, etc.</strong>)</li>
<li>🏗️ <strong>NestJS</strong> for backend and API development</li>
<li>🚀 <strong>Node.js</strong> as the runtime environment for scalability and performance</li>
<li>🤖 <strong>Telegram Bot API</strong> for seamless interaction with users</li>
<li>📊 <strong>Airtable Integration</strong> to manage and store user preferences and news content</li>
</ul>
<hr/>
<h2>Challenges we ran into</h2>
<p>Building <strong>SummerEase</strong> came with several challenges:</p>
<ul>
<li>📰 <strong>Scraping</strong>: Extracting relevant and accurate news data from various sources</li>
<li>🔗 <strong>Linking users to the bot and database</strong>: Ensuring seamless integration between Telegram users, our bot, and the database</li>
<li>⏳ <strong>Response time</strong>: Currently still long but with room for optimization</li>
<li>⚡ <strong>Parallelizing audio and text summary generation</strong>: Improving performance by generating both simultaneously</li>
</ul>
