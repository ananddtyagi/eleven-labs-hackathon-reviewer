<h3>What It Does</h3>
<p>Sun AI allows users to effortlessly convert articles or images into compelling video narratives. By inputting a URL or uploading an image, our AI agent analyzes the content and generates an engaging video using AI-powered narration and visuals.</p>
<h3>How We Built It</h3>
<p>We developed Sun AI using Next.js and deployed it on Vercel, integrating several powerful AI tools to enhance the experience:</p>
<p>ElevenLabs for high-quality, natural-sounding text-to-speech conversion.
Fal AI for AI-generated video creation.
Mistral &amp; OpenAI for advanced content understanding and processing.
Supabase for secure and efficient data storage.
Vercel AI SDK for seamless AI-powered interactions.
Challenges We Encountered
Building a smooth and efficient AI pipeline while maintaining video quality and real-time processing was a significant challenge. Optimizing the synergy between multiple AI models required careful engineering and testing.</p>
<h3>Accomplishments We Are Proud Of</h3>
<p>One of our biggest achievements is the high-quality, visually engaging, and immersive video generation our platform delivers. Sun AI transforms static content into entertaining, digestible, and shareable stories, making information consumption more enjoyable.</p>
<h3>What We Learned</h3>
