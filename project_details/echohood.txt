<h2>Inspiration</h2>
<p>Exploring a new city or moving to a new neighborhood can feel overwhelming. Traditional travel guides are generic, outdated, and lack relevance. Travelers, students, and new residents need real-time, personalized&amp;engaging insights to navigate their surroundings effortlessly.  </p>
<h2>What It Does</h2>
<p>EchoHood is an AI-powered hyperlocal audio guide that provides curated, location-based insights on:  </p>
<ul>
<li>Local landmarks and cultural hubs<br/></li>
<li>Multilingual support for global users<br/></li>
<li>Offline accessibility for uninterrupted exploration without roaming charges.</li>
<li>Personalized voices and themes for a tailored experience<br/></li>
</ul>
<p>Users can choose a neighborhood or enable location-based discovery to explore through AI-generated audio guides, categorized by interests such as history, restaurants, shopping and safety.  </p>
<h2>How We Built It</h2>
<p>We leveraged AI and machine learning to generate dynamic, context-aware audio content. The platform integrates:  </p>
<ul>
<li>LLM-based guides with relevant insights<br/></li>
<li>Multilingual speech synthesis for accessibility<br/></li>
<li>Custom voices generated with ElevenLabs </li>
</ul>
